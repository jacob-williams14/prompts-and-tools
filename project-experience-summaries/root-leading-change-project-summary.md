**Developer:** Jacob Williams

## Project Overview

Jacob Williams made significant technical contributions to the **Root Leading Change** project, a supplemental web application that served as a companion platform to Root Compass. While Root Compass was a comprehensive module-based online/in-person learning platform for global business leaders, Leading Change focused on providing ongoing growth resources including blogs, articles, podcasts, and other content to support continuous professional development after completing the Compass curriculum.

Williams demonstrated emerging technical leadership capabilities while delivering critical functionality for content management, internationalization, and platform modernization. His work spanned **full-stack development**, **DevOps infrastructure**, **quality assurance processes**, and **cross-functional collaboration** with design and content teams.
